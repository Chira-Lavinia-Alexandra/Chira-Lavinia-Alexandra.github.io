How to add a dash
of Wonderland in your life

Recently I started to read again “Alice in Wonderland”. Her marvelous adventures, that breach the logical rules of our mundane world, will never turn old and the story will continue to inspire youth around the world for decades to come. If you are passionate about this story and if you would like to spice up your everyday life with some magical elements of that world, you came to the right place.

If you are not a fan of creating outfits inspired from the book and of decorating your room/ house, I still have some ideas for you. Don’t worry, we are on the same boat.

Try to have some accessories related to the characters: a pocket watch, rabbit shaped rings, scarfs with the print of old written pages, pinz nez glasses or gloves.

For example, I have a really sweet white pen with a rabbit.

The easiest way out, when you want to look like Alice is to choose a blue or a yellow dress. If you like collars add one too. Don’t worry about your hair color. This aspect is not important. Even if Alice appeared to be blonde both in the illustrations and in the Disney Movies, the girl that inspired Lewis Carroll was a brunette little girl. Just add a bow in your hair.  

If you are more extravagant, why not carrying a flamingo with you?! alice-in-wonderland.net has a penchant for these extravagant ideas. Read more here

If the Mad Hatter was your favorite try wearing some oversized bowties, baggie pants or a jacket. Tim Burton’s movie changed the game when talking about the outfits inspired by the Mad Hatter.

The central idea is: Steampunk fashion. Try wearing some clothing elements from that area and, if you feel like it, don’t forget your hat at home.

I hope you found this article enjoyable!
