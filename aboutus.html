Our mission

books.x.cetera is here to help you during your journey as a reader. It doesn't matter if you are an experienced reader, or a person at the beginning of their journey still trying to make up their mind if reading is entertaining or not. I am here with reading tricks, book recommendations, and delicious recipes. Moreover, you can get involved too by sending me a dm on Instagram with a review of your favorite book.  All things considered, I wish you Happy Reading!

books.x.cetera FAQ

How can we send you our reviews?

My dear fellow reader, feel free to dm me on books.x.cetera with a review of your favorite book. Moreover, you can attach a picture of the book if you want to.

How often do you post on Instagram? 

I try to post regularly and share  new content at least 2 times a week. It takes time to take those pictures and to write the content. However, hopefully in the near future the number will go up to 3 posts per week. 

This bookstagram page has a commercial purpose?

No. The reason behind why books.x.cetera was created is to share my readings with you. I get so excited by some of my readings that I want to share them with the world.

Hi, my little moonflowers. I started this page as a little, semestrial project, but my intention for the future is to make this website and the bookstagram grow because I had so much fun while creating the content.

Who am I? I am just a reading enthusiast, a movie bluff with a photo camera at my disposal and good amounts of free time during the weekends. (It sounds like one of Professor Utonium's combinations).

I hope that you are going to enjoy it. Feel free to give me some feedbacks in the commentary section.
